Our Mission

Technology in Every Field

Crop diseases cause 40% of global food production losses every year. Early detection is the single most effective intervention — but traditional methods require expensive lab tests and expert knowledge most farmers can't access.

PlantCare AI democratises that expertise. By packaging a state-of-the-art convolutional neural network into a simple web interface, any farmer with a smartphone can get a professional-grade diagnosis in under 3 seconds — for free.

Dataset

Trained on PlantVillage

Our model was trained and validated on the industry-standard PlantVillage dataset, curated by researchers at Penn State University.

📁

87,000+ Images

High-resolution leaf photographs taken under controlled and real-field conditions across all 38 disease and healthy categories.

🔄

Data Augmentation

Each image was augmented with random flips, rotations, brightness shifts and cropping to improve model generalisation.

✅

Expert Validation

All disease annotations were verified by certified plant pathologists to ensure label quality and diagnostic accuracy.
