📸 Photo Tips for Best Results

Single leaf: Frame one leaf clearly, filling most of the frame.

Good lighting: Photograph in natural daylight. Avoid harsh shadows or glare.

Show symptoms: Make sure the discoloured or damaged area is clearly visible.

Clean background: Shoot against a plain, contrasting surface if possible.

Minimum 224×224 px: Higher resolution gives more accurate predictions.
